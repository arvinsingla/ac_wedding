A little about us

Andrew

Never did he ever think that marriage was right for him – nor did he think that he’d ever bump into an amazing, sassy and unique Texan lady who would turn his world and beliefs upside down – and in a cold place like Toronto no less. Apparently the universe had other plans because after two years of long distance – which entailed many Skype calls and monthly flights to and fro’ – he made his lady a special little pearl ring and asked her if she‘d make him sammiches for the rest of his life. She said yes.

Claudia

Always did she always know that her wedding would be a grandiose princess-like affair. We’re talking poofy cupcake dress, butler passed hors d'oeuvres and a spotlight on her coming down the stairs’ grandiose. There was only one missing puzzle piece – the gentleman. So she packed her warmest mittens and her sexiest snow boots and headed for Toronto. Call it karma or call it destiny, but she left three days later with that very puzzle piece who would later become her husband.

MIKE.

THE best man WHO’s going up against 8 bridesmaids

Besides being brave, Sir Michael is also wise – which is exactly why Andrew asked him to be the Best Man. As it happens, Andrew and Michael have been friends since the wee years in Jr. Kindergarten and they’ve literally known one another for over two decades.. That’s like almost how long it took for George Lucas to decide he wanted to ruin the Star Wars franchise with Jar Jar Binks.

In fact, our friendship has been so intertwined throughout our time here on this little blue planet that our mannerisms, tastes and even preference of video games are almost one in the same. I really had no idea what responsibilities went along with being a best man but for the reasons above as well as seeing that Mike is a well dressed gentleman scholar who knows his way around a The Google – I knew I’d be in great hands with this guy by my side on this expensive all important day.
